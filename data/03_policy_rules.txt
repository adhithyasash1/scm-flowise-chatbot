BQBYTE SUPPLIER GOVERNANCE POLICY RULES FOR RAG.

Tier Classification Rules:
Tier-1 Strategic suppliers:
- Annual spend above USD 5M.
- Minimum compliance score: 90.
- Minimum OTD rate: 93.0%.
- Maximum defect rate: 0.99%.
- Maximum payment terms: Net-90.
- Audit frequency: annual.
- Volume rebate eligible if Section 4.2 criteria are met.
- Alternate supplier recommended.

Tier-2 Preferred suppliers:
- Annual spend USD 1M to 5M.
- Minimum compliance score: 75.
- Minimum OTD rate: 84.0%.
- Maximum defect rate: 2.50%.
- Maximum payment terms: Net-45.
- Audit frequency: bi-annual.
- Alternate supplier mandatory.

Tier-3 Conditional suppliers:
- Annual spend below USD 1M.
- Minimum compliance score: 60.
- Minimum OTD rate: 75.0%.
- Maximum defect rate: 4.00%.
- Maximum payment terms: Net-30.
- Audit frequency: quarterly.
- Alternate supplier mandatory.

On-Time Delivery Rules:
- Tier-1 suppliers must maintain OTD at or above 93.0%.
- Tier-2 suppliers must maintain OTD at or above 84.0%.
- Tier-3 suppliers must maintain OTD at or above 75.0%.
- OTD is calculated on a rolling 90-day basis.

Defect Rate Rules:
- Tier-1 maximum permissible defect rate is 0.99%.
- Tier-2 maximum permissible defect rate is 2.50%.
- Tier-3 maximum permissible defect rate is 4.00%.
- Any single shipment with defect rate above 8.0% triggers immediate hold and RCA within 5 business days.

Supplier Watch List Rules:
- Any supplier with Compliance Score below 60 is placed on Supplier Watch List status.
- Supplier Watch List status limits new PO issuance to 20% of prior quarter volume.

Volume Rebate Rules:
- Only Tier-1 suppliers are eligible.
- Criteria: OTD at or above 93.0%, defect rate below 0.5%, and sustainability score at or above 85.
- Qualifying suppliers receive annual volume rebate of 2.5% of total annual invoice value.

Risk Rules:
- Low Risk: no active disruption flags, compliance above 85, no geopolitical exposure, diversified logistics.
- Medium Risk: geopolitical tension, seasonal disruption, pending regulatory audit, tariff adjustment, or compliance score 75 to 85.
- High Risk: two or more medium risk factors, active labour dispute, export control restrictions, currency volatility above 15% YTD, overdue audit, or compliance below 75.

Concentration Risk Rules:
- No single region may account for more than 45% of total annual procurement spend.
- No single country may account for more than 25% of total annual procurement spend.
- If concentration limit is breached, Procurement must submit Diversification Plan within 60 days.

Sustainability Rules:
- Tier-1 minimum sustainability score: 80.
- Tier-2 minimum sustainability score: 60.
- Tier-3 minimum sustainability score: 45.
- Suppliers below their tier minimum require Sustainability Improvement Plan within 45 days.

Certification Rules:
- ISO 9001 is mandatory for all suppliers in all tiers.
- Electronic Components require ISO 9001 and RoHS.
- Raw Materials require ISO 9001 and REACH for EU markets; ISO 14001 is required for Tier-1 Raw Material suppliers.
- Packaging Materials require ISO 9001 and FSC for paper-based packaging; ISO 14001 is required for Tier-1 Packaging Materials suppliers.
- Mechanical Components require ISO 9001; IATF 16949 is required for automotive use.
- Specialty Alloys require ISO 9001; NADCAP required for aerospace applications; AS9100 required for Tier-1 Specialty Alloys suppliers.
- Industrial Textiles require ISO 9001 and OEKO-TEX Standard 100.

Audit Rules:
- Tier-1 audited annually.
- Tier-2 audited every six months.
- Tier-3 audited quarterly.
- Suppliers with active disruption flag receive additional unannounced spot audit within 30 days.

Disruption Response Rules:
- Level 1 Monitor: Low Risk supplier with disruption flag. Weekly update and safety stock +15%.
- Level 2 Manage: Medium Risk supplier with disruption flag. Bi-weekly escalation calls, safety stock +30%, alternate supplier on 48-hour readiness notice.
- Level 3 Activate: High Risk supplier with any disruption flag, or any supplier with two simultaneous disruption flags. Immediate CPO escalation, alternate supplier activated for minimum 40% of volume within 10 business days, safety stock +50%, full RCA within 15 business days.
- Mandatory Level 3 regardless of risk level for export control restrictions, active labour strikes, regulatory enforcement actions, and port closure events exceeding 72 hours.

Alternate Supplier Rules:
- Every Tier-2 and Tier-3 supplier must have Alt_Supplier_ID.
- Tier-1 suppliers are strongly recommended to maintain an alternate.
- Alternate supplier must supply same SKU within 120% of primary supplier lead time, hold mandatory certifications, not share geopolitical risk region in Level 3 activation, and not be on SWL status.
